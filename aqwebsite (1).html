FAQ

Questions answered

What pricing structures do you offer?
+
We charge on an hourly basis. An hourly rate is agreed upon during scoping, and we also agree on time taken per task or transaction type — idle time is never charged to you.
What are the hourly rates?
+
Rates start from $10/hr and go up to $40/hr depending on the team composition required. We don't compete on price — we hire high-quality talent and deploy advanced technology. Exact rates are shared after a scoping call.
What if we're not happy with the service?
+
We offer the first 50 hours free to all new clients. If you're not satisfied within that window, you owe nothing. If your billing exceeds 50 hours in the first month and you're still unhappy, you're eligible for a full refund.
What is the billing & payment cycle?
+
We invoice on the 1st of the following month. Payment is due within T+5 days of the invoice date.
How do you ensure our data is secure?
+
We use best-in-class tools (Zoho Vault, Workdrive) for access management, data sharing and storage. Every file is tracked with controlled access. Strong internal walls and legal agreements ensure client confidentiality is maintained at all times.
Do you offer white-labelling?
+
Absolutely. We always communicate under your brand name and only act on your approval. Your clients will never know we're in the picture unless you choose to share that.
